Mon poem préféré:

There was a young lady of Niger,
Who smiled as she rode on a tiger;
They returned from the ride,
with the lady inside,
And the smile on the face of the tiger

Version alternative:

A hungry young lady from Niger,
Befriended a gullible tiger;
She started to chat,
But then swallowed the cat;
And the smile on the lady from Niger.
